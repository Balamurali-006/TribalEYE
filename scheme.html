PM-KISAN
Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi
Direct income support scheme providing ₹6,000 annually to small and marginal farmers to help them meet their agricultural needs and enhance productivity.
✓ Eligibility Criteria
  • Must own agricultural land
  • Land area should be ≤ 5 acres
  • Must be a small/marginal farmer
  • Land records should be updated
💰 Benefits
  • • ₹6,000 per year (₹2,000 per installment)
  • • Direct bank transfer (DBT)
  • • 3 installments per year
  • • No middleman involvement
📝 Application Process
  1. Visit nearest CSC or agriculture office
  2. Submit required documents
  3. Complete biometric verification
  4. Receive acknowledgment receipt
💧
Jal Jeevan Mission
Har Ghar Jal - Safe Water for All
Flagship program to provide safe and adequate drinking water through individual piped water connections to all rural households by 2024.
✓ Eligibility Criteria
  • Rural household with residential property
  • Agricultural land owners also eligible
  • Must not have existing piped connection
  • Community participation required
💰 Benefits
  • • Piped water connection to household
  • • 55 liters per person per day
  • • Quality monitoring and testing
  • • Maintenance support provided
📝 Application Process
  1. Contact Gram Panchayat
  2. Submit household survey form
  3. Community meeting participation
  4. Technical survey and connection
🔨
MGNREGA
Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act
Provides guaranteed 100 days of wage employment in a financial year to every rural household whose adult members volunteer to do unskilled manual work.
✓ Eligibility Criteria
  • Adult member of rural household
  • Annual income < ₹50,000
  • Willing to do unskilled manual work
  • Must register for job card
💰 Benefits
  • • 100 days guaranteed employment
  • • ₹200-300 per day wage
  • • Work within 5km of residence
  • • Skill development opportunities
📝 Application Process
  1. Apply for job card at Gram Panchayat
  2. Submit required documents
  3. Request work when needed
  4. Participate in allocated work
🏗️
DAJGUA
District Administration Job Guarantee for Unemployed Adults
Special employment guarantee program focused on tribal areas, providing extended employment opportunities and skill development for tribal communities.
✓ Eligibility Criteria
  • Annual income < ₹40,000
  • Own agricultural or barren land
  • Must be from tribal community
  • Age between 18-60 years
💰 Benefits
  • • Extended employment days
  • • Higher wage rates for tribal areas
  • • Skill development training
  • • Healthcare benefits
📝 Application Process
  1. Visit District Collector office
  2. Submit tribal community certificate
  3. Income and land verification
  4. Training and work allocation
